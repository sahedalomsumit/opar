Areas of Impact

Projects and programmes done by YAE-Canada, impact the following areas.

Education

Poverty reduction through the provision of quality education and skills training.

Agriculture

We equip and train youth to establish small-scale food processing & storage plants

Alternative Energy

Promoting the use of alternative sources of energy (other than wood) in order to conserve forests.

Conservation

We support youth in conserving mothernature and the environment they live in.
